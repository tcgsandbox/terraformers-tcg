A primordial wellspring suspended in a black void: a perfect column of bright, luminous water rising from a circular basin of carved silver-stone, soft motes of light drifting upward from it, no visible sky — only deep dark space around the spring. The first water in the world. Painterly fantasy illustration in a legendary, mythic style, brilliant white-gold light against deep blackness, glowing accents, square framing for a trading card.
